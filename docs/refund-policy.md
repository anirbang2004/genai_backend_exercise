# Refund Policy

This document describes refund eligibility for Northstar+ subscriptions and Northstar hardware purchases.

Because Northstar Fitness sells both digital services and physical products, the applicable refund or return path depends on what the customer purchased and when the request is made.

This document should be used together with the shipping and account support documents where relevant.

## Subscription refunds

Customers may request a refund for a **new Northstar+ subscription purchase within 30 days of the original charge date**.

This applies to the first qualifying charge for a new subscription purchase. Eligibility is evaluated based on the date the charge was processed and the surrounding account history.

Subscription renewal charges are generally **not refundable**, except where required by law or in cases of duplicate billing or clear billing error.

Examples of situations that may justify review include:
- duplicate charges for the same subscription period
- clear payment processing error
- legal obligations in a customer’s jurisdiction
- exceptional support-approved cases involving a documented platform issue

## Trial-related charges

If a customer started with a free trial and was charged after the trial ended, that charge is treated as a normal subscription purchase.

Refund eligibility is still based on the charge date and other conditions in this policy. A customer who forgot to cancel before the trial converted is not automatically excluded from eligibility, but the request is still assessed under the standard subscription rules.

## Cancellation and refund distinction

Refunds are not granted for partial use of a billing period after a cancellation request.

For example:
- if a customer cancels after a monthly plan renews, cancellation may stop the next renewal, but it does not automatically refund the already processed renewal charge
- if a customer cancels an annual plan several months into the term, the remaining unused portion is not automatically refunded

Cancellation prevents future billing. Refunds concern charges that have already occurred.

## Hardware returns and refunds

Physical devices such as the NS Band may be returned within **14 days of delivery** if they are in good condition and include original accessories.

Returned hardware should be sent back in a condition appropriate for inspection and resale unless the return is due to damage on arrival or another approved exception.

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless:
- the wrong item was shipped, or
- the product arrived damaged

A device that is damaged through misuse, unauthorized modification, or accidental impact may not be eligible for a refund.

## Return condition guidance

To support a hardware refund request, customers should return the device with:
- the charging cable
- original band, if available
- accessories included in the box, where applicable

Minor packaging wear does not automatically invalidate a return, but missing components or obvious damage may affect eligibility.

## How to request a refund

Customers should contact support with:
- the account email
- order number, if available
- reason for the request

Providing complete information helps reduce delays. In some cases, support may request additional documentation to confirm the account, purchase, delivery date, or payment details.

## Processing timeline

Approved refunds are typically processed to the original payment method within **5 to 10 business days**.

Actual posting time may vary depending on the payment provider or bank.

If a refund has been approved but does not appear after a reasonable period, the customer should first check with the payment provider and then contact support again if needed.

## Non-refundable scenarios

Refunds are generally not approved in the following situations:
- dissatisfaction after substantial use outside the eligible window
- hardware damage caused by misuse
- subscription renewal charges outside exception cases
- requests with insufficient evidence of purchase when verification is required

## Exceptions

This policy may be adjusted where local consumer protection laws require different treatment.

Northstar Fitness also reserves the right to review unusual situations individually, especially when there is evidence of platform error, duplicate billing, or order fulfillment mistakes.

## Priority of policy

If a more specific legal or regional requirement applies, that requirement may override the general rules summarized here.
