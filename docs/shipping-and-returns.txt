Northstar Shipping and Returns

This document describes the general shipping process for physical Northstar products and clarifies how shipping-related topics connect to hardware returns.

Northstar+ software subscriptions are digital services and are not shipped physically. Shipping timelines apply only to physical items such as the NS Band and related hardware orders.

Order processing:
Orders for physical products are typically processed within 2 business days.
Processing time may be longer during holidays, large promotions, inventory events, or periods of unusually high order volume.

Shipping speed:
Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days after dispatch.
Expedited shipping, where available, usually takes 2 to 3 business days after dispatch.

These delivery windows are estimates rather than guarantees unless explicitly stated otherwise during checkout.

Shipping destinations:
Northstar currently ships hardware to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, and Australia.

Shipping availability may change over time. If a country is not listed at checkout, shipping is not currently supported there.

Tracking:
Customers receive a tracking email once an order has shipped.
Tracking updates depend on the carrier and may not appear immediately after label creation.

If a package is delayed in transit, customers should first review the latest carrier update before contacting support.

Delivered but missing packages:
If a package is marked delivered but cannot be found, customers should wait 24 hours, check with household members or building staff, and then contact support.

In some cases, carriers mark a package as delivered slightly before the final handoff is completed. This is why Northstar asks customers to wait briefly before opening an investigation.

Incorrect address issues:
Customers are responsible for reviewing the shipping address before placing an order.
Northstar may not be able to reroute an order after it has entered carrier processing.

If an address error is discovered quickly, the customer should contact support immediately, but address changes cannot be guaranteed.

Returns:
Returns for physical hardware must follow the hardware return window described in the refund policy.
Returned devices should be shipped with the charging cable and original band if possible.

Customers should not assume that a return shipment itself guarantees a refund. Returned hardware is typically reviewed for eligibility and condition.

Exchanges:
Northstar does not support exchanges for color or size preference after shipment.
Customers who want a different version should return the original item if eligible and place a new order.

Damaged deliveries:
If a product arrives visibly damaged, customers should contact support promptly and include clear photos if requested.
Damage during transit may affect shipping fee treatment under the refund policy.

Relationship to refund policy:
The shipping document explains logistics timing and delivery expectations.
The refund policy controls refund eligibility, return windows, and reimbursement conditions.

Digital services:
Northstar+ software subscriptions are digital services and are not shipped physically.
Questions about subscription cancellation or subscription refunds should be handled under billing and refund documentation rather than shipping rules.
