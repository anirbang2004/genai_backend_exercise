# Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ summarizes common customer questions about Northstar products, subscriptions, shipping, and account actions. It is intended as a quick reference and should be read alongside more specific policy documents when necessary.

## Do I need a subscription to use the NS Band?

No. The NS Band can be used without a paid subscription for basic tracking features. However, premium coaching and advanced analytics require Northstar+.

Customers who stay on the Free tier can still pair the device, sync basic data, and review limited activity information in the app.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel from the billing section of the app or web portal. Cancellation stops future renewals.

In most cases, canceling does not end access immediately. Customers usually keep premium access until the end of the already-paid billing period.

## Does cancellation give me a refund?

Not by itself. Refund eligibility depends on the refund policy.

A canceled subscription may simply remain active until the paid term ends without generating further renewals. If a customer wants reimbursement for a processed charge, the refund policy controls whether that request may qualify.

## What is the refund window for a new subscription purchase?

Customers may request a refund for a new Northstar+ subscription purchase within 30 days of the original charge date, subject to the refund policy.

## Are renewal charges refundable?

Generally, subscription renewal charges are not refundable except in limited situations such as duplicate billing, billing error, or where required by law.

## How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days after dispatch. Expedited shipping, where available, usually takes 2 to 3 business days after dispatch.

Processing time before shipment is typically separate from transit time.

## Where does Northstar ship hardware?

Northstar currently ships hardware to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, and Australia.

## What if my package says delivered but I cannot find it?

Wait 24 hours, check with household members or building staff, and then contact support.

This recommendation exists because some carriers mark packages as delivered shortly before final handoff is complete.

## What if the app cannot find my NS Band?

Check that the band is charged, Bluetooth is enabled, and the device is close to the phone. If that does not work, restart the app or review the troubleshooting guide.

If the problem continues, the customer may also try restarting the phone and retrying pairing.

## Can I delete my account from the app?

Account deletion requests must be made through support.

Self-service settings are available for some account actions, but deletion is not handled as a normal in-app action in this dataset.

## Does Northstar sell health data to advertisers?

No. Northstar states that it does not sell personal health-related data to advertisers.

It may still share limited information with service providers involved in areas such as payment processing, hosting, analytics, and support operations.

## Can I return the NS Band?

Physical devices such as the NS Band may be returned within 14 days of delivery if they are in good condition and include original accessories, subject to the refund policy.

## Can I change the color or size after shipment?

Northstar does not support exchanges for color or size preference after shipment. Customers who want a different version should return the original item if eligible and place a new order.

## How quickly are approved refunds processed?

Approved refunds are typically processed to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days, though the exact posting time may depend on the payment provider or bank.
