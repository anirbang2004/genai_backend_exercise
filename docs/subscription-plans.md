# Northstar+ Subscription Plans

Northstar Fitness offers three Northstar+ software tiers intended for different levels of usage. The exact presentation of offers may vary by market or promotion, but the standard tiers are described below.

Northstar+ is a digital subscription service delivered through the Northstar app and related account systems. It is separate from the purchase of physical hardware such as the NS Band.

## Plan summary

Northstar currently offers:
- Free
- Monthly
- Annual

All paid plans renew automatically unless canceled before the next billing date.

## Free

The Free tier includes:
- basic activity tracking
- daily step summaries
- limited sleep history
- device syncing
- access to core account and device management tools

The Free tier is intended for customers who want to use the NS Band for basic tracking without committing to premium coaching or advanced analytics.

Customers on Free may still receive app prompts encouraging an upgrade to Northstar+ premium features.

## Monthly

The Monthly plan costs **$12 per month** and renews automatically every 30 days unless canceled.

Monthly plan features include:
- adaptive workout plans
- premium sleep insights
- recovery recommendations
- nutrition guidance
- weekly progress summaries
- guided training content

The Monthly plan is best suited for customers who want short-term flexibility or who are trying premium features before committing to a longer billing cycle.

## Annual

The Annual plan costs **$99 per year** and renews automatically once every 12 months unless canceled.

Annual plan features are the same as the Monthly plan, but the annual billing cycle offers a lower effective monthly price.

The Annual plan is designed for customers who expect to use premium features consistently over a longer period.

## Trial eligibility

New customers may receive a **7-day free trial** of Northstar+ premium features. Trial availability may vary by promotion or region.

Some offers may be shown only to new customers or only in selected storefronts. A trial offer may also be unavailable if the customer has previously used a similar introductory promotion.

If a customer cancels during the trial period, premium access continues until the end of the trial unless otherwise specified in a promotional offer.

A trial converts to a paid subscription unless the customer cancels before the trial ends.

## Billing behavior

Paid subscriptions renew automatically using the payment method on file unless the subscription is canceled before renewal.

Billing dates are generally based on the original subscription start date or the end of any applicable trial period.

Customers are responsible for keeping payment information current if they want to avoid interruptions, failed renewals, or accidental lapses in access.

## Cancellation

Customers can cancel their subscription from the account billing section in the mobile app or web portal.

Canceling stops future renewals, but it does not retroactively refund charges that have already been processed unless the request qualifies under the refund policy.

After cancellation, customers usually keep premium access until the end of the already-paid billing period. For example:
- a Monthly subscriber who cancels mid-cycle generally retains access until the current 30-day period ends
- an Annual subscriber who cancels before renewal generally retains access until the end of the current annual term

## Plan changes

Customers may see options to move between available plans. The details of upgrades, downgrades, and effective billing timing may depend on the account platform or purchase channel.

In cases where the app or billing interface offers a plan switch, the customer should review the displayed billing timing carefully before confirming the change.

## Failed payments

If a subscription renewal payment fails, the account may enter a short grace period before premium access is suspended.

During that period, the customer may be prompted to update billing information. If payment is not resolved, the account may revert to a non-premium state.

## Refund relationship

Cancellation and refunds are separate concepts.

Canceling a subscription:
- stops future renewals

Canceling a subscription does not automatically:
- reverse a processed charge
- guarantee a refund
- shorten the already-paid access period

Customers seeking reimbursement for a processed charge should review the refund policy and contact support if they believe their request qualifies.

## Availability note

Plan pricing, promotional offers, and billing flows may change over time. This document describes the standard plan structure used in the exercise dataset and should be read together with related policy documents.
