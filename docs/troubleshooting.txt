Northstar Troubleshooting Guide

This document lists common customer issues and practical first steps that can often resolve them without requiring support intervention.

The troubleshooting steps below are general guidance. If the problem continues after basic steps are completed, the customer may need to contact support.

Issue: The NS Band will not sync
Possible steps:
- Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on the phone.
- Keep the band close to the phone.
- Open the Northstar app and pull to refresh the device screen.
- Force close and reopen the app.
- Restart the NS Band.
- Check whether the app requires an update.

Additional note:
Sync issues are often caused by distance, Bluetooth state, or temporary app state problems. Retrying after an app restart frequently resolves the issue.

Issue: The battery drains too quickly
Possible steps:
- Reduce continuous heart-rate monitoring frequency if supported.
- Install the latest firmware update.
- Make sure the device is fully charged before long workouts.
- Remove and reconnect the device in the app if battery reporting seems inaccurate.

Additional note:
Battery performance can vary depending on usage patterns, sync frequency, firmware state, and enabled tracking features.

Issue: Premium features are locked after payment
Possible steps:
- Sign out and sign back in.
- Check whether payment completed successfully.
- Wait a few minutes for account status to refresh.
- Contact support if the account still shows the Free tier.

Additional note:
This issue may be caused by a billing delay, account mismatch, or incomplete payment confirmation rather than a device problem.

Issue: Sleep data is missing
Possible steps:
- Wear the band snugly overnight.
- Sync the device in the morning.
- Confirm the battery did not run out during the night.

Additional note:
If sleep data is consistently incomplete, customers should review fit and overnight battery level before assuming a sync defect.

Issue: App cannot find device during setup
Possible steps:
- Charge the band.
- Turn Bluetooth off and on again.
- Restart the phone.
- Keep the device next to the phone during pairing.

Additional note:
This issue is especially common during first-time setup if the band is low on power or the phone's Bluetooth state is unstable.

Issue: The app seems outdated or unstable
Possible steps:
- Install the latest version of the app.
- Close background apps if the phone is under heavy load.
- Restart the phone.
- Retry the action after reconnecting to the internet.

Issue: Account still shows Free after subscribing
Possible steps:
- Confirm that the purchase completed successfully.
- Make sure you are signed into the expected account.
- Sign out and sign back in.
- Wait briefly for account status to refresh.
- Contact support if the problem continues.

Issue: Device appears connected but data is old
Possible steps:
- Pull to refresh the device screen.
- Keep the phone and band close together.
- Confirm Bluetooth is still enabled.
- Reopen the app.
- Restart the band if necessary.

When to contact support:
Customers should contact support if:
- purchase status looks incorrect after basic checks
- delivery problems require investigation
- account recovery is needed
- a refund request must be reviewed
- the issue persists after the documented troubleshooting steps
