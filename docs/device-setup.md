# NS Band Setup Guide

Use this guide to set up a new NS Band and connect it to the Northstar mobile app.

This guide focuses on first-time setup, pairing, syncing expectations, and a few common issues that can occur during onboarding.

## Before you begin

Make sure:
- the NS Band is charged
- Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
- the Northstar app is installed
- you are signed in to your Northstar account

For the smoothest setup experience, keep the device near the phone and make sure the phone has internet access during initial configuration.

## What you need

Before starting, the customer should have:
- the NS Band
- a compatible smartphone with the Northstar app installed
- login access to the correct Northstar account
- enough battery charge on both the phone and the band

If the customer has multiple Northstar accounts, they should confirm they are signed into the intended one before pairing.

## Pairing steps

1. Open the Northstar app.
2. Tap **Add Device** from the device screen.
3. Hold the side button on the NS Band for 3 seconds until the pairing icon appears.
4. Select the NS Band when it appears in the app.
5. Confirm the pairing code if prompted.

After successful pairing, the app may begin syncing device information automatically.

## Sync requirements

The NS Band syncs using Bluetooth. The phone should remain within normal Bluetooth range during pairing and sync.

A stable internet connection is recommended during initial setup so the app can finish account verification and download updates.

Bluetooth is required for direct communication between the phone and the device. Internet connectivity is recommended for account services and update checks, but the most important local requirement for pairing is that Bluetooth is enabled and the band is nearby.

## Firmware updates

During setup, the app may install a firmware update. Keep the band near the phone and do not close the app until the update completes.

Interrupting a firmware update can delay setup or require a retry.

If a firmware update takes longer than expected, the customer should first confirm that:
- the phone remains close to the band
- the app is still open
- Bluetooth remains enabled
- the band has sufficient battery charge

## Account connection

The device is associated with the signed-in Northstar account used during setup.

If a customer pairs the band while signed into the wrong account, they may need to remove the device and repeat setup after switching accounts.

## Common setup issues

If the app cannot find the band:
- confirm Bluetooth is on
- make sure the band is charged
- restart the app
- move the band closer to the phone

If pairing appears to start but does not complete:
- keep the phone and device close together
- confirm the pairing code if prompted
- avoid switching away from the app during setup
- restart the app and try again

If setup still fails, consult the troubleshooting guide.

## Setup environment tips

For best results:
- avoid pairing in areas with many active nearby Bluetooth devices if interference is suspected
- place the phone and band next to each other during initial setup
- avoid starting setup when the band battery is nearly empty
- allow enough time for firmware updates on first use

## After setup

Once setup is complete, the customer can:
- review activity summaries
- sync recent data
- access supported app features
- manage subscription and account settings
- begin using premium coaching features if subscribed

If premium access does not appear after purchase, the issue may be related to account status rather than device pairing alone. In that case, the troubleshooting or billing guidance may be more relevant than the setup guide.
