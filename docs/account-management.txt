Northstar Account Management

This document describes common account-related actions customers can take directly and identifies cases where support involvement is required.

Customers can update their profile, password, and billing settings from the account area of the app or web portal.

Profile management:
Customers can review and update basic profile information using self-service account settings where available.

Subscription cancellation:
A customer can cancel an active Northstar+ subscription at any time before the next renewal date.
Cancellation prevents future renewals but does not immediately revoke access already paid for in the current billing period.

This means a customer who cancels usually retains premium access until the end of the current paid term, unless a separate billing issue changes account status.

Email changes:
If a customer no longer has access to their old email address, they should contact support for account recovery assistance.

Support may ask for information needed to verify ownership before making sensitive account changes.

Password reset:
Customers can request a password reset from the sign-in screen.

If the customer no longer has access to the email inbox associated with the account, normal password reset flow may not be sufficient and support may be needed.

Deleting an account:
Account deletion requests must be submitted through support.
Deletion of an account does not automatically guarantee immediate deletion of all retained records if retention is legally required.

Billing issues:
Customers who believe they were charged in error should contact support and include the account email and transaction details.

Providing complete billing details can help support determine whether the issue involves:
- a normal renewal
- a duplicate charge
- a payment processing problem
- a misunderstanding about cancellation timing

Self-service vs support:
The app or web portal is generally suitable for:
- profile updates
- password changes
- cancellation
- reviewing billing settings

Support is generally required for:
- account deletion
- account recovery without email access
- manual refund review
- investigation of unusual billing issues

Account ownership:
Customers should keep account credentials secure and ensure that device pairing and subscription activity occur under the intended account.
Some issues that look like payment or access problems are actually caused by signing into a different account than the one used for purchase.
