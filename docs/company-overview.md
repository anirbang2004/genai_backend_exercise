# Northstar Fitness Overview

Northstar Fitness is a fictional health technology company that offers a wearable fitness tracker called the **NS Band** and a digital coaching subscription called **Northstar+**.

The company focuses on helping customers build sustainable fitness habits through a combination of device-based activity tracking, in-app coaching, recovery insights, and structured training guidance. Northstar Fitness serves individual consumers who want lightweight health insights without the complexity of a clinical platform.

Northstar's product experience is built around two connected parts:

1. **NS Band**, a wrist-worn device that captures movement and other wellness signals.
2. **Northstar+**, a software subscription that provides coaching features, advanced analytics, and guided plans in the mobile app.

The company sells directly to customers through its online storefront and supports users through help documentation, self-service account management tools, and email-based support.

## Mission

Northstar Fitness aims to make fitness and recovery guidance more accessible by offering simple, actionable recommendations instead of overwhelming dashboards or expert-only terminology.

The product is designed for customers who want:
- day-to-day activity tracking
- structured workout guidance
- basic recovery awareness
- sleep trend visibility
- a simple app experience tied to a wearable device

## Product ecosystem

### NS Band

The NS Band is a lightweight wearable designed for all-day use. It tracks:
- heart rate
- step count
- sleep duration
- workout activity
- estimated recovery score

Depending on app settings and firmware support, the device may also participate in syncing activity summaries and other wellness indicators to the customer's account.

The NS Band connects to the Northstar mobile app using Bluetooth. The device is intended for consumer fitness use only and should not be considered a medical device.

### Northstar mobile app

The Northstar mobile app is the main companion experience for the NS Band. Customers use the app to:
- pair and manage devices
- review recent activity
- inspect sleep trends
- manage subscriptions
- update account settings
- access premium coaching content

The app also serves as the main point for premium feature delivery. Customers with no paid plan can still use the app for basic activity summaries and device syncing.

### Northstar+

Northstar+ is the software subscription that unlocks premium in-app features such as:
- adaptive workout plans
- guided training programs
- deeper sleep trend analysis
- recovery recommendations
- premium progress reports
- nutrition guidance

A customer may own and use an NS Band without subscribing to Northstar+, but some in-app coaching and analytics features remain locked unless premium access is active.

## Who Northstar serves

Northstar products are aimed at general fitness consumers rather than professional athletes or healthcare providers. Common customers include:
- people trying to become more active
- customers returning to fitness after a break
- casual runners and gym-goers
- users interested in sleep and recovery habits
- people who want app-based structure and reminders

Northstar does not position its product as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Sales and support model

Northstar Fitness sells hardware and digital subscriptions separately.

Important distinctions:
- the **NS Band** is a physical device purchase
- **Northstar+** is a recurring software subscription
- refunds and returns for hardware and subscriptions may follow different rules
- support interactions for billing, returns, and access issues may require account verification

Customers can browse help documents, manage billing through their account settings, and contact support by email for issues involving refunds, defective devices, and account access.
