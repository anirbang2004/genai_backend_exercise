# Privacy Summary

Northstar Fitness collects account, device, and usage information to provide the service.

This summary is intended to explain, at a high level, the kinds of information Northstar may process and why that information is relevant to the product experience.

It is a simplified overview for convenience and does not replace a full legal privacy notice.

## Types of data collected

Northstar may collect:
- account information such as email address and subscription status
- device identifiers
- workout activity
- sleep metrics
- app interaction data
- customer support communications

Depending on the product flow, Northstar may also process operational information associated with account access, billing status, device pairing, and service diagnostics.

## Why data is collected

Data is used to:
- operate and improve the service
- generate activity insights and recommendations
- process subscriptions and purchases
- respond to support requests
- detect fraud or abuse

Some of this information is needed for core functionality. For example:
- account information supports sign-in and billing status
- device data supports syncing and customer insights
- support communications help resolve customer issues

## Product personalization

Northstar uses collected data to generate activity summaries, progress trends, and customer-facing recommendations inside the app.

This use is meant to support the product experience rather than to provide medical conclusions.

## Data sharing

Northstar does not sell personal health-related data to advertisers.

Northstar may share limited information with service providers that support:
- payment processing
- cloud hosting
- analytics
- customer support operations

These providers may process data on Northstar's behalf for service delivery purposes.

## Customer support and investigations

When a customer contacts support, Northstar may use account, order, and service history information to:
- verify identity
- investigate billing concerns
- review refund eligibility
- troubleshoot device or subscription issues

## Account deletion

Customers may request account deletion through support.

Some transactional records may be retained where required for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or accounting purposes.

Deletion of an account request does not necessarily mean immediate removal of every record connected to the account if retention is required for legitimate legal or operational reasons.

## Security and limitations

Northstar states that it uses service infrastructure and operational controls to support product security, but no internet-connected platform can guarantee absolute security.

Customers should use a strong password and protect access to their account credentials.

## Important limitation

This summary is provided for convenience and does not replace the full privacy notice or applicable legal terms.

Where legal requirements differ by jurisdiction, those requirements may control how data rights and obligations are applied.
